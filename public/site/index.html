About Fly91

We fly where bigger jets don't.

Fly91 started in Goa in 2024 with one idea — that India's tier-2 and tier-3 cities deserve honest, direct flights to the big ones. No changeovers. No 14-hour drives. Just a short hop on an ATR 72-600, on a timetable built for how people actually travel.

We're small on purpose. Smaller planes fit the smaller airports that bigger carriers won't touch. Sindhudurg. Agatti. Jalgaon. These are the routes that matter, and we run them properly.

i First time flying?

A few things to know.

For first-time flyers, nervous flyers, and the curious.

Why do ATR planes sound louder than jets?
Because they're turboprops — propellers you can see, and your seat is closer to them. The sound is mechanical, not a sign of stress. Same aircraft flown across regional Europe, Japan, and North America.
What is turbulence, actually?
Air isn't flat. When warm and cool air meet — near clouds, mountains, coastlines — it feels bumpy. Aircraft are built for far more than you'll ever feel on a Fly91 flight.
Do small planes feel different from big jets?
A little. You'll notice takeoff more, feel the air more, the cabin is cozier. That's it. ATRs are purpose-built for shorter regional hops.
What if weather changes mid-flight?
The pilot has three options: hold nearby, divert to a better airport, or turn back. All three are routine. You'll hear from the flight deck, and from our voice support when you land.
